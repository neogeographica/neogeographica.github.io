Steamed Myth

  • playing Myth:TFL and Myth II on the Steam Deck
  • originally published November 11, 2022

The Myth games from Bungie were my other "hobby games" during the 90s, and I still revisit them occasionally. My most recent run was after getting a Steam Deck; for whatever reason, I decided to see if Myth could be properly installed/configured on the Deck and set up with a workable control scheme. The answer is yes! (IMO)

Not to bury the lede too much, the main purpose of this post was really just to link to the MythDeck documentation that I put together. But since I was rescuing some old cohost posts, I figured I'd grab this one too.

Originally posted on the social blogging site cohost.org; the cohost theming is mostly preserved here.
